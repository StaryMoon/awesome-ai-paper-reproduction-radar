# 3D, Gaussian, and Rendering Pack

For readers connecting 3D generation, Gaussian splatting, inverse rendering, and embodied scene construction.

**Good for:** 3D vision students, graphics readers, and embodied AI researchers who need a cross-paper map.

**Why now:** 3D repos are often hard to compare because each paper uses different asset, rendering, and representation assumptions.

3. Use the later entries to compare adjacent tasks, data assumptions, or evaluation setups.
