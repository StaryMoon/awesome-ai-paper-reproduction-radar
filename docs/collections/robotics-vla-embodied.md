# Robotics, VLA, and Embodied AI Pack

For readers following vision-language-action models, bimanual manipulation, dexterous grasping, and inertial robot positioning.

**Good for:** Robotics students, embodied AI builders, and people comparing action-conditioned model interfaces.

**Why now:** Robotics papers are getting pulled into foundation-model discussions; a focused track makes this transition more legible.
