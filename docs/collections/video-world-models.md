# Video Generation and World Model Pack

For readers watching text-to-video, camera control, delta-token world models, and physical-AI video priors converge.

**Good for:** Video generation builders, world model readers, and robotics people who need visual dynamics references.

**Why now:** Video/world-model papers are search-heavy but scattered; a curated track makes the field easier to scan.
